About Me

My Mathematics Journey

I started my Maths Olympiad Journey in elementary school. I have participated in various competitions since then. Some of my proudest achievements are the medals I've won in the Indonesia National Math Olympiad (OSN). I won Absolute Winner in elementary school and middle school. I've also won two silver medals in the high school division. This year, I ranked #8 on the Indonesian TST for the 2025 IMO. I'm currently once again participating in the TST for the 2026 IMO. I also have some experience teaching Olympiad Mathematics.

School Organizations

During middle school, I started to get interested in organizations and joined my school's Student Council (OSIS) for one year. I learnt a lot of things during my time there. Therefore, in high school I decided to once again join a student's organization. I joined the Student's Representative (MPK) starting from 10th grade. In 2025, I got elected as the President of the Student's Representative for the following year.

Community

Early 2025, me and my friends decided to start a non-profit organization called Project SPARK. Here, we give back to our community by going to orphanages and spreading awareness for environmental sustainability. During the year, we have done a lot of projects such as PINTAR, GROW, and so on. I'm truly grateful to everyone involved in this project.

Personal Life

Throughout the years, I've also done some other things. I've been playing the piano since I was little albeit not too seriously. I can speak some Mandarin. I also learned how to read and write Hiragana, Katakana, and Hangul. Other than that, I picked up some basic coding since the year 2024.
